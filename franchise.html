Why Us?

Al-Baik.Com is a well recognized franchising company all around India. Every AL-BAIK.COM restaurant in India and Nepal are owned by our Franchisees.

In 2009, Al-Baik.Com opened its first restaurant in Lucknow, and since then we have opened 85+ restaurants all around India and Nepal. Our restaurants are located in Lucknow, Kanpur, Gorakhpur, New Delhi, Mumbai, Nepal, Bihar, Kolkatta and many others parts of India.
